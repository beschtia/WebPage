Something about me

Hi. My name is Davor Lukić and I am 35 years old. I was born in Požega, but now I live in Našice. Most of my childhood I spent in Čaglin, where I lived with my family. There, I finished primary school.
I moved to Našice in 1997 when I started my high school education. I finished gymnasium. My majors were Math, Science and Physics.
In 2001 I started going to college. I studied Psychology, but I haven`t finished. I decided that it wasn't the right path for me so I dropped out. I wanted to get a job and start to earn money, to be independent. Unfortunately, that didn't happen. After a few years, I finally found a job. However, it was only a part time job so I couldn’t earn much, but it was enough to get by.
After a while, I started thinking what next. I was wondering what my dream job might be, what and where I would like to work. So, it finally hit me. I have always loved computer science and I am a huge fan of electronics. I maintain and repair my neighbors’ computers all the time. I know basic programing and I wanted to learn more. So, here I am.
I am a third year student of Electrical Engineering and Computing, working part-time, a husband and a father to a daughter. It's quite demanding but, with love and help of my family, I manage. I never thought it would be easy, and believe me, it surely isn't.
